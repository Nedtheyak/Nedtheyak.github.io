Dressember 2020

I'm doing a campaign where I wear a tie all day every day in December, raising awareness and money for the International Justice Mission (IJM). They work to rescue people from human trafficking and slavery, and have been doing so for over 20 years.

There are about 40 million people being trafficked right now, and we can change that.
